Extension options

Learn how to setup your webserver and use Xdebug : Learn more.

  • XDEBUG_SESSION Cookie

    Xdebug cookie to allow you debugging of your application at runtime. This is the most common usage and is always enabled. IDE key should match your server configuration.

  • XDEBUG_TRACE Cookie

    Adds a new icon to your toolbar to enable tracing.

  • XDEBUG_PROFILE Cookie

    Adds a new icon to your toolbar to enable profiling. Use it with a tool like KCacheGrind.
